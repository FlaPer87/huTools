Other Tools related to huToolsÂ¶

We have developed a suite of tools which extend hutools for our dayly use.

  • huBarcode - barcode Generation
  • pyJasper - PDF generation with iReport and JasperReports
  • pyBookkeeping - access to SWIFT and other Banking standards and to the Xero API
  • pyShipping - shipping related calculations and routing Information for DPD/Geopost
  • pyPostal - printing and sending of letters through Pixelletter
  • EDIlib - various EDI/data exchange formats
  • huSoftM - access to the huSoftM ERP System
  • huDjango - mist helpers for the Django Framework
  • huImages - high preformance image serfing stack
  • django-googleappsauth - authentication for Django against Google Apps
